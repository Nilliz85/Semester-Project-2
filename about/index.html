About

Welcome to my online auction platform! This was developed as part of my front-end development studies at Noroff School of Technology and Digital Media. The project's purpose was to utilize JavaScript techniques to build a front-end for an auction application using an API provided by Noroff.

This platform is for users with @noroff.no or @stud.noroff.no email addresses. After registration and profile creation, users can log in to access features like listing items for auction, bidding on items, and viewing auction history.

In addition to the mandatory features, there are optional functionalities like adding comments to listings, setting reserve prices, and following other users. The aim was to build a functional yet appealing auction platform that demonstrates both the required and optional features effectively. Enjoy your experience on the platform!
